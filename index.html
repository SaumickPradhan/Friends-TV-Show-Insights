"Friends" is a popular American sitcom that aired from 1994 to 2004 on NBC. It consists of 10 seasons with a total of 236 episodes. The show revolves around the lives of six friends living in Manhattan: Rachel, Ross, Monica, Chandler, Joey, and Phoebe.
